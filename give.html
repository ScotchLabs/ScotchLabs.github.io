We appreciate any and all donations from students, alumni, and fans of Scotch'n'Soda Theatre. Donations can be made through Carnegie Mellon Giving to the organization to help fund our efforts and activities, to our alumni organization to support alumni engagement, or to our endowment.

The university will not recognize designations of specific subtroupes of Scotch'n'Soda Theatre. If you would like to give directly to a subtroupe, please make a donation towards Scotch'n'Soda Theatre and email snstheatre.md@gmail.com with information about your donation and where you would like it to go.





ABOUT US

Scotch'n'Soda is a student-run theatre organization at Carnegie Mellon University. Its initial dedication was the creation and production of original musicals. Currently students are welcome to write, compose, design, direct, perform in, and otherwise become involved with every aspect of each production.


The organization is open to all Carnegie Mellon students from all backgrounds, and all performances are public with varying ticket prices.
