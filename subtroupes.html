TISBERT

Sketch Comedy

Tisbert is Carnegie Mellon’s premiere Sketch Comedy Troupe! We have weekly writing meetings open to all CMU students, leading up to our semesterly show. Currently, meetings take place Sundays from 3:30 to 5PM in Baker Hall 255B. At Tisbert, writers and actors of all skill levels are welcome! We also teach a StuCo (Student Taught Course): 98-003 "Introduction to Sketch Comedy" every Wednesday from 6:30 to 8:20PM in Wean Hall 5310. Visit us at our Facebook page and join our D-List.

NO PARKING PLAYERS

Improv Comedy

The No Parking Players is a long form improv troupe, performing hour-long shows every other week in the CUC Studio Theater. We teach a "Fundamentals of Improv" StuCo as well as a supplementary workshop that meets every Thursday and Sunday, respectively. We started going to festivals in the spring of 2016, and have since performed at SteelStacks in Bethlehem, PA, the Del Close Marathon in New York City, and a number of college festivals. We are proud of our growing relationship with nearby theaters (Arcade and SCIT), our supportive alumni, and fellow college troupes across Pennsylvania and Ohio. Visit us at our Facebook page and our website, and join our D-List.

NewWorks Coffeehouse

Workshopping

NewWorks CoffeeHouse is all about student written work. We give young playwrights the opportunity to have original works of theatre produced, providing the campus with experimental and new theatre. NewWorks is also dedicated to improving and editing works in any way that serves the population of passionate playwrights, whether through workshops or individual meetings. Visit us at our Facebook page and join our D-List..

ABOUT US

Scotch'n'Soda is a student-run theatre organization at Carnegie Mellon University. Its initial dedication was the creation and production of original musicals. Currently students are welcome to write, compose, design, direct, perform in, and otherwise become involved with every aspect of each production.


The organization is open to all Carnegie Mellon students from all backgrounds, and all performances are public with varying ticket prices.
